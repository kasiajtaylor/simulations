How it Works

Our tool simplifies the process of binary to decimal conversion by multiplying each binary digit by the power of 2 corresponding to its position and summing the values.

For example, to convert 1010, the tool calculates: (1 * 2^3) + (0 * 2^2) + (1 * 2^1) + (0 * 2^0) = 8 + 0 + 2 + 0 = 10.
